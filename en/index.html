Frequently asked

What book do I need?

The numbers point into Cocktailian — Das Handbuch der Bar (Adam/Hasenbein/Heuser, Tre Torri Verlag, ISBN 9783944628868). If the book already lives behind your counter — perfect. If not: book first, app second. Without the manual, Bibelstunde doesn't make much sense.

Does the app work offline?

After the first login the browser caches the app and you can keep using it without a connection. Draws, filters and lucky numbers run purely in the browser.

What device do I need?

An iPad or Android tablet is best — flat behind the counter, always in fullscreen. Works just as well on any phone or laptop with a browser.

How do I get access after buying?

Currently by email within 24 hours — Stripe checkout with instant delivery follows in the next few days.

Multiple bars — one account?

No — each bar gets its own access so your team doesn't accidentally draw from the wrong pool. Multiple bars? Multiple purchases, or email me for bundle rates.

Can I add my own cocktails?

For now the pool is curated and fixed — custom recipes are planned as a premium feature in an upcoming version.
